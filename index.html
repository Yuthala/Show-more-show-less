After the Hurons left, Uncas went and spoke with Hawkeye.

“What does Hawkeye say?” asked Uncas.

“Give me twenty rifles; then you go and find them, and we will attack them. After that, we will take the woman from his cave.”

So, twenty warriors were selected, including David and Duncan, to follow Hawkeye, and they walked to an area close to the beaver lodge. Here they would make the first attack against the Huron. The two groups left together and went in their different directions. When Hawkeye and his men reached the beaver lodge, they were pleased to see Chingachgook and Munro join them.

The Hurons soon appeared nearby in an open area. A shout came from Uncas as he came from the other side of the forest leading a hundred warriors. Hawkeye and his men fired at the Huron, and many of their warriors fell, while some of them began to run away.
